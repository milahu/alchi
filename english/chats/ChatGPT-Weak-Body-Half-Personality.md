## 1. Manual Laterality (Handedness)

**What it is:** Preference for one hand for skilled, precise actions.

## 2. Footedness

**What it is:** Preference for one foot in actions requiring precision or support.

**Two subtypes (important!)**

- **Manipulative foot** (skill): kicking a ball

**Simple tests**
