# Tribalism

1 tribe = 150 humans.

Our planet can support [3 million tribes](carrying-capacity.md).

Each tribe has:

- 150 humans
- 300 cows
- 1500 goats
- 1800 sheep
- between 1,000 hectares (10km²) grazing land in humid climates
  and 20,000 hectares (200km²) grazing land in dry climates

The annual slaughter rate is around 20%.
Every week, each tribe will slaughter:
1 cow, 10 sheep, 10 goats.
