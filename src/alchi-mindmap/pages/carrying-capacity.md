# Carrying capacity

How many humans can we feed on our planet?

If all humans want a [natural lifestyle](natural-lifestyle.md),
then our planet supports only 500 million humans.

Currently we have about 10,000 million humans
= 20x too many
= [95% must die](depopulation.md).

The number 500 million humans is the result of this formula:

1. Every human needs around 0.5 kg meat per day (1% of his body weight).
2. We have 3,000 million hectares of pasture land.
3. Extensive agriculture produces 60 kg meat per hectare per year.
    - 20 kg in dry climates
    - 400 kg in humid climates with Holistic Planned Grazing by Allan Savory

3 E9 * 60 = 180 E9 kg meat per year.

0.5 * 360 = 180 kg meat per year per human.

180 E9 / 180 = 1,000 million humans.

With a safety factor of 2, we arrive at 500 million humans.

Georgia Guidestones, first commandment:
"Keep the population under 500 million."
