Preparing to head back to ‘Stone Age’

The surging retail price of gasoline has prompted the Royal Thai Police in Lampang province in Thailand return to the ‘Stone Age’ as police officers are testing the use of horse drawn carriages as part of their patrolling exercise.

Horse drawn carriages are a unique symbol of the province but the police patrol has never been one of the tourist attraction of the province.

The mad king of the west (Donald Trump) had issued ultimatums to Iran that he would bomb that country back to ‘Stone Age’ but the ripple effect of the blockade of Strait of Hormuz seems to have made other countries suffer the consequences and Lampang is already testing how it feels like going back to ‘Stone Age’.
