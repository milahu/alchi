Modern anxiety epidemic explained:

You're constantly exposed to global problems you cannot influence, watching curated lives you can't replicate, absorbing outrage designed to keep you engaged, and measuring yourself against impossible standards.

The Stoics would diagnose this immediately: You've lost the distinction between what you control and what you don't.

Relearn it or stay miserable.
