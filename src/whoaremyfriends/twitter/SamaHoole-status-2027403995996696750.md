The drugs most commonly prescribed in the UK deplete the nutrients most critical to the conditions they treat.

Statins: deplete CoQ10. CoQ10 powers the mitochondria of the heart they're meant to protect.

Metformin: depletes B12. B12 deficiency causes neuropathy. Diabetic neuropathy is the leading cause of non-traumatic amputation. The drug for diabetes depletes the nutrient whose deficiency causes one of diabetes's worst complications.

PPIs: deplete magnesium, B12, calcium, zinc. Magnesium deficiency causes muscle cramps, arrhythmias, and anxiety. The stomach acid drug causes the symptoms that lead to other prescriptions.

Loop diuretics (for heart failure and hypertension): deplete potassium, magnesium, thiamine. Thiamine deficiency causes cardiac dysfunction. The heart drug depletes the vitamin required for cardiac function.

Oral contraceptives: deplete B6, B12, folate, magnesium, zinc, selenium.

Corticosteroids: deplete calcium, potassium, magnesium, zinc, B6.

Antacids (aluminium-containing): deplete phosphate, calcium.

Every drug that inhibits an enzyme depletes something the enzyme was producing.

The depletion is documented.

The monitoring and replacement are not standard.

The depletion becomes a symptom.

The symptom becomes a prescription.

The second prescription depletes something else.

The map is not hidden.

It is in the literature.

It is not in the appointment.
