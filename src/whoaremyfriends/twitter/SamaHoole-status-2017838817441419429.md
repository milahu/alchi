What actually heals your gut lining:

- Glycine (from collagen)
- Proline (from collagen)
- Glutamine (from meat)
- Vitamin A (from animal fat)
- Vitamin D (from animal fat)
- Butyrate (produced when you're fat-adapted)

What irritates your gut lining:

- Fiber (mechanical irritation)
- Lectins (creates permeability)
- Phytates (inflammation)

Doctors recommend the second list.
